Glassmorphism Cards
rgba backgrounds with subtle border opacity. Creates depth without heavy shadows. Native iOS feel.
Status-Colored Left Borders
Green = new/selected. Blue = queued. Gray = already contacted. Status understood in under 1 second.
XP Bar Gamification
Orange → yellow → green as quota fills. Glowing dot tracks progress. glowing dot completes at 20/20.
Gradient CTAs
Green→cyan gradient on primary actions with glow shadow. Orange gradient on Search. Hierarchy instantly clear.
Contact History
Your Outreach is the history. Sent coaches stay in it permanently with their status chip. No separate history screen needed — scroll your Outreach to see who you contacted, when, and what sport.
Weekly Quota · 20 Emails / Week
Show the quota counter on the search screen, the results screen, and the home screen. User always knows how many emails they have left before searching — never hits the wall mid-send.
Outreach Naming
Auto-name every Outreach from the search filters: Baseball · D-I · Utah Outreach. User can tell all their Outreaches apart at a glance. No more "20 Coaches · UT."

What Needs to Change — 9 Issues

Green = already exists and is good · Orange = in progress · Red = bug to fix · Blue = new feature

01Bug
"Search Coaches" silently creates an Outreach
Tapping Search fires two things: returns results AND creates an Outreach. Coaches already in any prior Outreach for that sport+state are permanently excluded from results.
Search = browse only. Show ALL coaches with prior-contact status chips. Explicit "Start Outreach" creates it — user decides, not the search.
Francis (query logic) + Badinho (decouple search from Outreach create)
02Bug
19 coaches disappear after 1 sent
Outreach detail only shows Sent section. Queued = 0 (wrong). No Continue button.
Full Outreach always shown. Status chip per coach. "Continue Outreach (19 left)" CTA.
Francis (query fix) + Badinho (UI)
03Bug
Outreach names have zero context
"12 Coaches · UT" — can't tell what sport or why it exists
Auto-name Outreach from filters: "Baseball · D-I · Utah Outreach"
Francis (store sport/div) + Badinho (display)
04Bug
Pending counter shows 0 (wrong)
Shows 0 Queued even with 19 unsent coaches in the Outreach
Pending = all coaches not yet marked Sent. Must be accurate.
Francis
05In Progress
No Outreach count badge on home screen
No indicator on home that active Outreaches exist
Badge count on Outreach tile showing active + queued — already being built
Badinho (in progress)
06New Feature
No auto-advance after send
After "Yes, I sent it!" → goes back to Outreach detail. Stranded.
After confirmation → auto-show Preview for next coach in the Outreach.
Badinho
07New Feature
No way to delete an Outreach
Outreaches pile up forever with no cleanup
Swipe left on Outreach card = delete with confirm dialog.
Francis (endpoint) + Badinho (swipe UI)
08New Feature
Weekly quota invisible
User doesn't see 20/week limit until they hit it
"X of 20 used" bar on search + results + home screen.
Francis (data) + Badinho (UI)
09Bug
"Mixed" gender is ambiguous
Mixed = "no gender" AND "coach covers both" — two different things
Force gender select. Mixed = dual-gender coach flag. WHERE sport_gender IN (:gender, 'mixed')
Francis (query) + Badinho (force select UI)
